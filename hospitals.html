ℹ️ How does a facility get listed here?
1
Fill the onboarding form
Hospital or facility fills a simple form on Curaah with their basic details. Takes under 5 minutes.
2
We verify and connect
Curaah team visits within 24 hours, verifies the facility, and sets up their profile and attendant portal for free.
3
QR codes generated & profile live
Unique QR check-in codes generated for each OPD department. Print and stick them at entrance — done. Profile goes live instantly.
4
Patients book & check in
Patients book OPD appointments online, scan QR at arrival, get token number, sit comfortably — no queues.
