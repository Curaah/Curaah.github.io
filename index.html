How It Works

From prescription to recovery — 4 steps

Designed for elderly patients who have never used a smartphone, and powerful enough for tech-savvy caregivers who want full control.

1
Register & Build Your Profile
Tell Curaah about your age, health condition, lifestyle, and existing medicines. AI builds your complete health context — stored securely so you never repeat yourself.
2
Upload Your Prescription
Take a photo of your prescription. AI extracts every medicine, dosage, and timing automatically. Pharmacist verification adds clinical accuracy before your plan is created.
3
AI Companions You Every Day
Timely reminders, diet guidance, exercise advice. Ask the AI anything in your language. Log vitals. Upload lab reports monthly. Your family monitors your progress in real-time.
4
Walk Into Your Doctor With Data
Generate a comprehensive health PDF — adherence record, vitals history, lab trends — at any time. Your next appointment becomes a productive conversation, not a memory test.
