Our Story

A patient spends 45 minutes
in the healthcare system.
And 23 hours alone.

Curaah was founded from a simple observation: healthcare doesn't fail at diagnosis. It fails during recovery — the 23 hours and 15 minutes every day that patients spend outside the healthcare system.

spent alone every day

During those unsupported hours, medicines are forgotten. Diets are ignored. Reports are misunderstood. Follow-ups are missed. Lifestyle recommendations are abandoned. Family members remain uninformed. Doctors lose visibility into patient progress.

The result is that millions of patients continue to suffer despite receiving medical treatment.

We believe the future of healthcare is not only diagnosis and treatment. The future is Recovery Intelligence, Healthcare Continuity, Personalized Health Guidance, and Data-Driven Recovery Outcomes.

Our Mission
To create a continuous healthcare ecosystem where patients, families, doctors, healthcare facilities, and public health systems remain intelligently connected through data, AI, and personalized recovery support.

Curaah Recover — Core Features

Everything your recovery needs

Every patient is unique. Two patients with the same disease need different recovery strategies. Curaah creates personalized recovery journeys — not generic health recommendations.

🧬
Digital Health Twin
Every patient builds a living, evolving health profile — your age, gender, conditions, prescriptions, medication history, lab reports, lifestyle habits, recovery trends, adherence behavior, and family support system. The AI continuously learns from your interactions and health data, developing a deep understanding of your unique recovery journey.
Core Philosophy
💊
Medication Intelligence Engine
Upload prescriptions, photograph medicine strips, scan tablets. AI verifies correct medicine, dosage, timing, prescription compliance, potential interactions, and duplicate therapies. Pharmacy-independent — works with medicines from any pharmacy.
AI Vision
🤖
AI Recovery Companion
Unlike generic chatbots, Curaah's AI already knows your medications, diseases, reports, and progress. Ask "Can I eat Chinese food today?" — the AI evaluates your disease, medication profile, recent health data, and recovery goals before responding.
Contextual AI
📊
Recovery Tracking & Visualization
Track blood sugar, HbA1c, blood pressure, weight, SpO2, laboratory markers, adherence scores, and recovery milestones. Beautiful visual dashboards help you understand whether recovery is actually happening — converting healthcare into measurable progress.
Core Feature
🍱
Personalized Recovery Planning
Recovery depends on nutrition, exercise, sleep, mental wellbeing, and lifestyle habits. AI generates personalized recommendations based on your disease, medications, health profile, recovery stage, geography, and environment — not generic internet advice.
AI Personalization
🔬
Lab Report Explainer
Upload lab reports. AI explains HbA1c, creatinine, cholesterol, and 50+ values in simple words your family can understand. Track how your condition improves over months of treatment.
Core Feature
🚨
SOS Emergency System
One tap sends GPS location + full medical record to family and 108 emergency services. DPDP 2023 compliant consent gate. First responders get your medicines, allergies, and conditions instantly.
Emergency Ready
⚠️
Side Effect AI Triage
Report any side effect — AI classifies severity (green/yellow/red), explains possible causes, suggests safe home remedies for mild cases, and tells you exactly when to seek emergency care.
AI Triage
📄
Doctor-Ready Health PDF
One-click PDF with full recovery summary — medicines taken, adherence percentage, vitals history, lab trends, side effects. Every doctor visit becomes a productive, data-driven conversation.
