ℹ️ How referrals work on Curaah
1️⃣

Doctor fills digital referral

Your doctor enters diagnosis, urgency level, and required department on Curaah and books the referred hospital appointment simultaneously.

2️⃣

You get referral + appointment together

You receive your digital referral letter and appointment confirmation in one SMS. No separate visits, no paper letters to carry.

3️⃣

Your records travel with you

The referred hospital receives your complete health history digitally before you even arrive. No files to carry. No information lost.

📟 How the token system works
📱

Scan QR at hospital entrance

Show the QR code from your appointment SMS at the kiosk. Token assigned in seconds. No standing in registration queue.

🪑

Sit and relax

No standing in OPD queue. Watch the live token display screen or track your position here. You will get an SMS when you are next.

🔔

Get notified when your turn is near

SMS alert when you are 2 tokens away. Walk to the OPD calmly. No rushing, no missing your turn.

👤

No phone? No problem

Face recognition kiosk at hospital entrance checks you in using Aadhaar face match. Or hospital staff can check you in by entering your phone number.
