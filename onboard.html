Once your hospital is onboarded, Curaah generates unique QR codes for each of your OPD departments. You simply print them and stick them at the OPD entrance — no hardware, no installation, no electricity needed. When a patient arrives with a Curaah appointment, they scan the QR code on their phone and get a token number instantly. They sit down comfortably and receive an SMS notification when their turn is approaching. For patients without smartphones, your staff can check them in on any device. The entire setup costs nothing and eliminates OPD queues from day one.
